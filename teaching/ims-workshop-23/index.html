Workshop on Conversational AI (Winter Semester 2023/24)

Jul 21, 2023
credits: xkcd

Description

The workshop offers an immersive learning experience centered on Conversational AI, guiding participants through the process of developing their own chatbot and encouraging them to reflect on the issues related to human-machine interaction that arise when developing chatbots.

Participants

  • Students of MA Interactive Media Systems and European Project Semester (max 15)
  • Language: English
  • Prerequisite: Basic programming skills, interest in language-based human-machine interaction
  • Bring your own laptop

When & Where

Monday, 4. December 2023
Tuesday, 5. December 2023
Friday, 8. December 2023

TH Augsburg Master Interactive Media Systems
